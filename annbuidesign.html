Hi, I’m Ann Bui





My name is Ann Bui and I am a graphic designer based in
Winnipeg, Manitoba. Coming from Vietnam, I was driven by
my burning passion for design.

When designing, I value an inseparable connection
between aesthetic and functionality. It’s fascinating to design
with a strategy in mind, considering how the human mind works
and how people interact with design.

I work with a crafter’s spirit. From idea generation to the
production of a well-designed piece, with one obsession:
the quality of the execution.

Above all, I’m always open to new experiences and
creating positive change.
